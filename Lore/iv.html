Recap of Chapter III

At a shop, the player bought a card. It mysteriously had a GPS code at the back. The player and his friend work together to decipher such thing. It didn't take very long. The duo discovers a secret laboratory one mile from the office. They go there. They meet a friend of the shopkeeper back at the shopkeeper. At the lab, the shopkeeper's friend notices the duo got the card. He shows the duo a device he invented called Neuralink. It has the power to trick the boss into giving the player a raise, but it will get more expensive over time. It also takes a few hours to work. The next day, the player goes to work. When his shift ends, the boss tells him he got a raise. The player leaves work proud.

It has been a few days since the last chapter. The player has used Neuralink a few times since then. He's at home right now.

I love Neuralink, makes me rich!

I wonder if there are any other cards in store...

Ima go there after lunch...

After lunch, he goes to the card shop place again.

Hello there, how can I help you?

Are there any new items in stock?

Wait a sec... you look familiar!

Uh-huh! I was your second customer!

That's great to know! And we do have new cards today.

Seems like there are only a few cards...

Oh! I have a cool card for you!

So what does it do?

Gives you a drone that's only good at one thing...

What's the one good thing?

Ha... you'll see...

Oh, you secretive shopkeeper...

It costs 200 points.

The player pays the money.

Go on, find out what it is!

OK...

Bye!

The player leaves the shop, excited to find out what he meant by the drone.

Lemme see if his friend could help...

The player calls the shopkeeper's friend.

What's good bro!

Hi!!

I have the second card, he said it had something to do with a drone.

A drone, huh?

Yep, a drone.

Come to my lab again, I'll show you what he means.

Coming!

The player arrives at the lab.

Okay, so about the drones, why did you bring me here?

You need me to activate the drone.

Why did that shopkeeper give me a broken drone anyway?

IDK, that's just him!

Now can you fix this thing up?

Why not?

Great! Does it cost anything?

Yes. 100 points actually.

Oh wow! Pretty expensive...

I promise you it's totally worth!

OK, but just curious, can I buy many of these drones?

Nope, but you can upgrade this drone!

Great! Now, what's the max level?

You can upgrade only 5 times!

Oh, cool!

He pays 100 points. The shopkeeper's friend gives the drone to him.

OMG I'm so excited!!!

The next day...

OK, little drone-y, go and type for me!

The drone drives to the player's workplace and starts typing, but slowly.

Hours later, the boss calls the player.

Excuse me, but why did some drone take your place today?

I just had to be creative, and no one said it's against the rules to do that!

Wait what? I'm sure there was a rule about such thing...

The boss hurrily checks the rule book.

OMG you're right!! What a creative person you are!

Ah thanks, bye!

Bye!

The player hangs up.

Wow what such creativity! If only everyone else did that...
